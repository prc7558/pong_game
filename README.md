# 🎮 Pong Game

A classic Pong game built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Play against a computer opponent and try to reach 11 points first!

## ✨ Features

- **Player vs Computer**: Challenge the AI opponent
- **Mouse & Keyboard Controls**: Use mouse movement or arrow keys to control your paddle
- **Smooth Ball Physics**: Realistic ball movement with bouncing and spin mechanics
- **Collision Detection**: Accurate collision detection for paddles and walls
- **Scoreboard**: Real-time score tracking
- **Game Status**: Visual feedback during gameplay
- **Responsive Design**: Beautiful gradient background and modern UI
- **Win Condition**: First to 11 points wins the game
- **Game Reset**: Easy reset functionality to play again

## 🎯 How to Play

### Controls for Player 1 (Left Paddle)

- **Mouse Control**: Move your mouse up and down to control the left paddle
- **Keyboard Control**: 
  - `↑ UP Arrow` - Move paddle up
  - `↓ DOWN Arrow` - Move paddle down

### Game Rules

1. The game starts with the ball in the center
2. The computer controls the right paddle automatically
3. Use your paddle to hit the ball back to the opponent
4. Score a point when the ball passes the opponent's paddle
5. First player to reach **11 points** wins!
6. Press `R` key or click "Reset Game" button to restart

### Ball Physics

- Ball bounces off the top and bottom walls
- Ball bounces off both paddles with spin effect
- Spin increases based on where the ball hits the paddle
- Ball speed gradually increases during rallies
